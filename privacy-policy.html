1. Purpose

This Privacy Policy explains how VA Solutions LLC collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when you use SWAP. SWAP is a user-to-user marketplace for giving away or swapping items. The app includes account sign-in, location-based listings, photos, chat, reports, moderation, notifications, and profile features.

2. Information we collect

Account and sign-in information. We collect email address and authentication identifiers from email OTP, Google, or Apple sign-in. We also process provider identity information needed to keep your account linked to the correct sign-in method.

Profile information. We collect full name, profile photo, phone number if provided, city, region, country, postal code, and approximate geohash location. Public profile details may include name, avatar, general location, rating, reviews, and completed deal count.

Listing information. We collect listing title, description, item type, category, condition, photos, location, status, listing dates, listing reports, moderation status, and internal moderation signals.

Chat and transaction information. We process messages, image attachments, conversation metadata, read status, deal completion status, feedback, and reports about listings, messages, or users.

Device, technical, and usage information. We process device type, operating system, app version, network state, crash logs, diagnostics, security events, and push notification tokens when notifications are enabled.

3. Location data

SWAP uses location to show relevant listings and to help users create listings in the correct area. You can allow device location access or enter location information manually. The app does not need continuous background location tracking for the core marketplace experience. Listings and profiles may show general location information such as city, region, or country, but not precise GPS coordinates.

4. How we use information

  • Create and manage user accounts and profiles.
  • Show listings, search results, filters, favorites, reports, and listing details.
  • Enable chat, attachments, deal completion, ratings, and reviews.
  • Send service messages, push notifications, and moderation decisions.
  • Detect fraud, spam, prohibited content, abuse, and security issues.
  • Provide support, investigate reports, enforce the Terms, and comply with legal duties.
  • Maintain app performance, fix bugs, and improve reliability.

5. Legal bases where required

Where privacy law requires a legal basis, we process personal information based on contract, legitimate interests, consent, or legal obligation. Consent may apply to optional permissions such as push notifications, device location access, and photo library access where required.

6. Content moderation and safety

SWAP may review listings, photos, messages, and reports to detect prohibited or unsafe content. Moderation can include automated checks, keyword rules, third-party moderation services, and manual review. Listings may be hidden, rejected, archived, or held for verification.

7. Sharing of information

We share information only as needed to operate SWAP, protect users, or comply with law.

  • With other users: public profile data, listing content, chat messages, reviews, and general location shown in the app.
  • With service providers: hosting, authentication, database, storage, analytics, crash reporting, notifications, geocoding, and moderation providers.
  • For legal and safety reasons: where required by law or needed to investigate abuse, fraud, threats, or rights violations.
  • Business transfers: if SWAP or VA Solutions LLC is involved in a merger, sale, financing, or restructuring.

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary meaning of selling user data for money. We also do not knowingly share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

8. International transfers

SWAP is intended to operate worldwide, with focus on Western Europe and North America. Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live. Those countries may have different data protection laws. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

9. Retention after account deletion

After account deletion, we delete or anonymize account profile data within 30 days, unless we need to keep limited information for legal, security, fraud-prevention, dispute-resolution, or abuse-prevention purposes.

We may retain reports, moderation records, safety records, and related audit logs for up to 24 months after account deletion. If a legal dispute, investigation, law enforcement request, fraud case, safety incident, or abuse investigation is active, we may retain relevant information until the matter is resolved and for a reasonable period afterward. Backup copies are deleted through normal backup rotation.

If SWAP later introduces paid services, tax, accounting, or payment records may be kept for the legally required period.

10. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, restrict, object to, or withdraw consent for certain processing of your personal information. You may also have the right to appeal a privacy decision or complain to a data protection authority.

If you are in California, Canada, the United Kingdom, the European Economic Area, or another region with specific privacy rights, we will handle your request according to the laws that apply to your situation.

11. Security

We use technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. No system is fully secure. You are responsible for keeping your sign-in method and device secure.

12. Children

SWAP is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly allow users under 18 to create accounts. If you believe a person under 18 is using SWAP, contact us so we can review and take action.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. If changes are material, we will provide notice through the app or another reasonable method. The updated policy will show a new effective date.

14. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact VA Solutions LLC at swapper.adm@gmail.com. Registered location: Tbilisi, Georgia.
