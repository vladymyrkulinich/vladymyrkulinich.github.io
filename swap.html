Main features

Designed around simple local exchanges

SWAP combines listing creation, location filters, direct chat, deal completion, reviews, and safety controls in one mobile flow.

G

Give and swap listings

Users create item listings with photos, title, description, category, condition, and location.

L

Location-based discovery

Feed results can be filtered by city, country, distance, type, category, condition, search, and date order.

C

Chat between users

Each exchange can continue in a listing-based chat with messages, image attachments, and read state.

R

Deal completion and reviews

Both sides can confirm a completed deal and leave feedback to build trust inside the product.

M

Reports and moderation

Listings can be reported and moderated to reduce scams, offensive content, and prohibited items.

S

Secure sign-in

SWAP supports email OTP and provider-based sign-in through Google and Apple.

P

Profiles and ratings

Users have public profile information, completed deal counts, ratings, and review history.

N

Notifications

Users can receive app notifications for messages and important listing or moderation events.
